American Institute for Chemical Engineers AIChE

According to their website, the American Institute for Chemical Engineers is the world's leading organization for chemical engineering. Consisting of more than 50,000 members from 100 different countries, AIChE posses many resources that are beneficial to its members including but not limited to updated chemical information, global networks, and learning opportunities.
